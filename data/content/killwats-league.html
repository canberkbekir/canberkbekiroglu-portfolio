Fast-paced 1v1 sports game with dynamic cameras and arcade ball physics.

About the Project

Killwats League is a competitive 1v1 sports game built in Unreal Engine. Players face off in fast-paced matches with dynamic camera systems and arcade-style ball physics that keep the action intense.

My Role

As Gameplay Developer at Futuregames Warsaw, I was responsible for implementing core gameplay mechanics, the camera system, and multiplayer networking.
